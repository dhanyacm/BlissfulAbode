About

A

place where you can have a holiday of the soothing kind, where you can unwind and enjoy nature's gifts - leaving behind the hustle and bustle of daily life.

You can choose to lie in or wake up early with the birds. You can walk amid the lush green paddy fileds or stroll along the spice plantation or go trekking in the adjoining hills.. You can catch fish from huge pond in the premises or catch a glimpse of unmatched Kerala theatre - there is no dearth for activities here

The family house set on four acres of lush evergreen with its exquisite ancient architecture that dates back over a hundred years, built with traditional Kerala wood of Rose wood & Teak which is the home of your hostess and host This is a functioning farm - with Paddy, Pepper and spices as the main crops.
